How we'll fund our plan

Politic's plan will invest in making New Zealand a better place to live, work, visit, and do business. Our policies will see major investments in housing, health, education, police, and infrastructure.

Housing

We are currently low on funding so we will be making 50,000 less houses. We will still be making 50,000 homes.

Health

Increasing government funding for all practices that lower their fees by $10, lowering the average non-VLCA fee from $42 to $32 and the maximum fee from $69 to $59

Clean Rivers

Clean water is the birth-right of all of us, and vital to our continued prosperity. Our rivers and lakes are a taonga of huge significance to Māori, a favourite place of recreation for New Zealanders. Water underpins our agriculture.

Transport

Build light rail from the CBD to Auckland Airport. This will be part of a new light rail network that will be built over the next decade with routes to the central suburbs, the airport, and West Auckland, and will later be extended to the North Shore
